Not For Everyone

This program is for planners who already know what they're doing.

FirstEventFree.com isn't a hand-holding onboarding experience. It's for experienced event professionals who want to evaluate a serious platform by actually using it — not by watching a demo.

If you need someone to walk you through every step, we have a demo process for that. This program is for the planners who'd rather just build.
